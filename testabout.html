About Us

"We are a team of open-source enthusiasts who would love to bring to the world the power of open-source software by delivering high-quality, versatile open-source applications which can be applied in any job sector in the global market. We believe that inclusion is key: everyone has the ability to build and develop your own software, as well as distribute it globally for everyone to use. We strongly support one single fact: software should be built by the people, for the people."
